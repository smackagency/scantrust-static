Why secure QR codes are the future of anti-counterfeiting: webinar recording

Peter Kostur, Sales & Partnerships Director EMEA, walks you through some of the pros and cons of different types of anti-counterfeiting technology; covert vs. overt and physical vs. digital, emphasizing secure QR codes.

How do you protect your customers, brand, and products from counterfeiting? What is important for such solutions that provide that protection? Four words: security, usability, scalability, and utility. There are many physical and digital anti-counterfeiting technologies available, but which is the right one for your company? In a presentation by Peter Kostur, Sales & Partnerships Director EMEA at Scantrust, Peter walks us through some of the pros and cons of different types of anti-counterfeiting technology. You’ll be taken through:

  • covert vs. overt
  • physical vs. digital
  • and the role that secure QR codes play in protecting customers, brands, and products.

Secure QR code benefits highlighted in this presentation are:

  • Instant authentication result
  • Familiarity and ease of use for consumers – largely based on universal QR code usage
  • Most smartphones scan and authenticate secure QR codes without a special app
  • Seamlessly connect with existing marketing and supply chain tools
