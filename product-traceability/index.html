How it works: Scantrust product traceability

1. Unique IDs applied to packaging

Serialized IDs or secure QR codes are applied as labels or printed directly on products; these can also be RFID, NFC; or lasered.

2. Associate data to product IDs

Codes or chips on products have data updated by scanning systems or smartphone apps.

3. Professional or end customer scans the code or reads the chip

QR code or RFID, NFC is scanned within supply chain, by distribution partner, or by end-user.

4. See and act on connected packaging data
