Scantrust wine labels satisfy strict E.U. requirements while being quick and easy to setup

Scantrust e-Label software is especially designed for wine makers and sellers selling wine in the European Union. Get in compliance with the E.U. agricultural reform regulations using this turnkey digital label solution.

Get the e-Label solution

Why choose Scantrust for E.U. wine labels?

Satisfy E.U. nutrition label requirements for wine: no additional labels to print or attach needed

Compliance with E.U. restrictions that come into effect December 2023 prohibiting data collection and sales/marketing content

Quick and intuitive setup using the Scantrust e-Label editor to get digital labels online in minutes

Generate digital nutrition labels linked to QR codes or a URL on wine bottles and packaging

Ingredients available by electronic means
Scantrust e-Label offers two options for including the wine ingredients and nutrition information required in the E.U. starting December 8, 2023. The first is a unique QR code for each SKU or wine product. The second is a dynamic URL website address. In either case, the information satisfies requirements with setup and hosting included with your subscription to the Scantrust e-Label software solution.

 

No additional tags or labels
By adding an electronic ingredient and nutrition label, use more of your on-bottle label for branding, product design, or other messaging. You also won’t need to print and add additional labels which incur additional costs and can be damaged or removed.

Strict compliance with a quick setup and reliable hosting

Restrictions on data collection
User behavior data gets collected by web pages and apps, like Google Analytics, commonly used in company websites. Because this collection is a violation of the new E.U. regulations for wines, we Scantrust e-Label for E.U. wines ensures compliance with technology that carefully avoids violations.


The required information, nothing more
The latest E.U. Common Agricultural Policy changes define that no sales or marketing information is allowed alongside mandatory ingredient and nutrition details delivered electronically. Violations can result in removal of products from their intended market. Scantrust e-Label ensures that your wines are in compliance and only link to the required, restricted content.

 

Prepare and insert electronic nutrition label data quickly and easily. Launch a digital product label in mere minutes
Scantrust e-Label software is designed to make short work of filling in the details for wine products. You can issue the QR code or URL right from the interface and integrate it with your label design as needed. 

How do you setup wine labels using Scantrust e-Label for E.U. wine labels?

1. QR codes are issued through the e-Label software

Once you’ve filled in product details, you’ll be able to issue and download your product page-specific QR code or URL

2. Codes/URLs are integrated into wine label design

Unique label designs can be prepared without an ingredient and nutrition table, so long as they feature the necessary QR or URL

3. No nutrition information on the label, no problem

All required ingredient and nutrition information in a digital format that meets legal requirements.
