News: Scantrust SA is Now an SAP Partner

Scantrust continues collaboration with SAP by joining the SAP PartnerEdge Build program

BERLIN, Germany, November 12, 2019 — Scantrust SA team members met with SAP stakeholders, mentors, potential clients, and investors over several months during the summer of 2019 SAP.iO program. While participating in SAP.iO, the three Scantrust solution categories were presented to SAP clients including patented authentication, supply chain intelligence, and consumer engagement. A significant share of the program was dedicated to technical integrations which included putting Scantrust technology together with S/4 Hana, the SAP ERP solution for large enterprises, and SAP Marketing Cloud, a contextual, real-time marketing suite. With the SAP.iO program complete, Scantrust most recently joined the PartnerEdge Build Program, further demonstrating a commitment to collaborate and innovate as a partner of SAP and to better serve Scantrust and SAP clients. This step will help Scantrust continue to build traction within SAP and drive projects together with SAP and serves as a great example, of how SAP.iO supports start-ups within the SAP ecosystem.

“Integrating with SAP is an opportunity to scale our business by tapping into the SAP client base. At the same time, we’re providing new services to Scantrust clients and that adds to what sets Scantrust apart from our competitors. Participating in SAP.iO gave us momentum which culminated with the SAP integrations that we have now.” – Nathan Anderson, Scantrust CEO

“Scantrust is an example of how we can bring in new, innovative Start-ups into our ecosystem via SAP.iO to extend our offering to our customers. We saw a great fit from the start and we will continue to support Scantrust at identifying valuable opportunities to scale.” – Alexa Gorman, Head of SAP.iO Foundries EMEIA

“Scantrust was introduced to me via SAP.iO and I immediately saw a great fit for our customers in Consumer Products. Our customers have been looking for ways to build new channels to communicate and engage with their consumers, build trust by showing them the provenance of their products, and get insights on those consumer interactions. This is what Scantrust provides. The fact that Scantrust is integrated with SAP makes them that much more appealing to our customers.” – Mark Osborn, VP, Strategy & Operations, SAP Consumer Products Industry Business Unit

About SAP.iO:

SAP.iO is SAP’s strategic business unit to accelerate innovation and drive new business models for SAP. The SAP.iO Fund makes investments in early-stage startups building innovative solutions that enable customers to receive highly incremental value from SAP platforms and applications. This allowed Scantrust to become an SAP partner. The SAP.iO Foundries are a global network of equity-free accelerator programs in strategic startup hubs providing startups access to mentorship, exposure to SAP technologies, and opportunities to meet and collaborate with SAP customers. Learn more at https://SAP.iO/.

About Scantrust:

Scantrust was founded in 2014 and provides secure product digitization to brands worldwide.

Scantrust is a connected goods platform for transforming everyday products and things into new sources of data. We enable anti-counterfeiting and supply chain traceability for goods and products using QR codes, RFID, and NFC tags. Our solutions help maintain your brand’s integrity, deliver valuable consumer insights, and create new opportunities for consumer engagement. Learn more at www.scantrust.com.
