Scantrust E-label General FAQs: questions and answers

What is the Scantrust e-Label solution?
Scantrust e-label is a software tool for sharing and managing product information associated with a product through labeling or packaging. Scantrust e-label can be used to generate the QR codes that can then be placed on labels as well as the websites that are associated with those QR codes. The benefit of this is to provide product information to end-customers without having to share the all details, whether mandatory or otherwise, on labels or tags. Due to strict regulations and requirements, especially for wines destined for sale in the EU, the Scantrust e-label solution also helps companies to be in compliance by adhering to technical requirements such as data management particulars or avoidance of prohibited content.

Companies using Scantrust e-label are getting the best solution for handling product information through digital means while having confidence that they are compliant with the most current EU requirements.

Who can benefit from using the Scantrust e-label solution?
Wine & spirit producers selling in the EU market need to monitor and understand EU regulations and requirements for product labels. The latest regulations and requirements reflect notable changes and they continue to evolve.

The Scantrust e-label solution is designed to benefit companies that want to simplify their compliance efforts or improve their workflow for managing product label production that satisfies EU requirements. Wine sellers in particular are liable for compliance with new label information requirements from December 2023 and onward. Spirits sellers do not have the same requirements yet, but they are advised to watch the wine sector as an indication of any upcoming changes for their products.

Is Scantrust e-label just for wine producers?
Scantrust e-label is not just for wine & spirit producers. Beverage distributors, printing partners, label providers and printers, and wine and spirit associations all stand to benefit from the turnkey Scantrust e-label solution and experience.

Is Scantrust e-label relevant for spirits producers?
Yes, the Scantrust e-label solution is an excellent option for spirits producers. Spirits labels do not have the same requirements as wine labels in the EU but the benefit of a QR code end-customer experience remains for spirits sellers. One of the main benefits of Scantrust e-labels is that each product can be associated with a customized landing page. Scantrust e-label landing pages can be launched in minutes with custom text, visual elements, promotional or other informational content, and they have uptime guarantees as well. Scantrust has years of experience helping companies as a QR code generation engine with printing integration for existing workflows. Scantrust e-label was built to integrate into existing printing processes, whether offset, flexo, gravure, or using variable data printing, to give you the benefits only available from links and QR codes on packaging. Spirits producers have a lot to gain by using the Scantrust e-label on their bottles and packaging labels.

If I already use QR codes on my bottles, why do I need the Scantrust e-label?
Scantrust e-label works best and most conveniently with our complete suite as one-stop solution for QR codes on bottles and packaging. By using Scantrust e-label, you can have greater certainty that your QR code and product associated landing pages are in full compliance with requirements. Some of those requirements are very strict. In the case of EU wine label requirements, it is prohibited to collect personally identifiable information or to serve marketing content to end customers alongside mandatory ingredient and nutrition information. Uptime is also a concern that e-label improves on. Finally, the management of QR codes, from generation to printing, is a process that is centralized and streamlined by Scantrust software.

Will consumers have to pay to access the e-label webpages and information?
No. At least for wine sold in the EU, it is unlawful to charge end customers for access to mandatory product information.

Who can use Scantrust’s e-Label platform to create e-Labels?
Scantrust e-label is especially designed for businesses that sell wine and spirits. For access to the Scantrust e-label platform, including free-tier access to the e-label platform, you can visit the Scantrust website.

Does the Scantrust e-Label solution only offer support for English?
Scantrust e-label is designed to work for all 24 official EU languages. The user experience is such that the language viewed for any product’s page will depend on the end-customer’s locale.

 

Scantrust e-label product specific FAQ: questions and answers particular to the software service

Do you have an energy calculator?
Yes! You can enter the alcohol strength, amount of sugar, organic acid, we calculate the energy value for you, based on EU conversion factors.

I do not know the amount of sugar, organic acid… for energy calculation
The industry also allows using commonly accepted values based on alcohol strength. We plan to offer it as a feature after we confirm it is accepted by the regulation

I have many SKUs….
We allow you to duplicate E-label information for similar SKUs. We are working on providing a mass upload option to upload information in bulk.

Can I print the code first and upload the information later?
Yes, it’s a dynamic code, you can modify both the E-label information and the landing page (Premium and above) anytime and anywhere. You can even modify the destination URL of the code.

What languages do my customers see on the landing page?
We have two options: your customer will see it in their device language or in the official language of the country in which the customer is located. We support all EU official languages.

What if the customers see some fields which are not using the industrial jargon…
We continue to evolve our product and we like to work with our customers on the improvement. Please share your feedback with us.

Can I design my QR code as fancy as Jacob Creek’s QR code?
Right now there is no regulation on the appearance of QR codes, so you can design your QR code any way you like. It is industry practice to put an “i” logo in the middle of the code to let the consumers know they can find product information by scanning. However we would advise not to make the QR code too fancy that it appears like a marketing code.

Can I put the QR code on the bottle neck?
Right now there is no regulation on the location of the QR code, but it is advisable that (1) the QR code are not torn OR removed after opening the bottle (2) to place the QR code close to other information, e.g. energy value, serving portion, which is usually in your back label

Can I print a QR code and stick it on the bottle, instead of changing my back label?
Right now there is no regulation that specifies the QR code has to be printed on the label, so this is doable. Note that it is pretty common right now that the QR code is on the back label along with other product information and make sure that the QR code is always available on the bottle for the consumers to access the information.
