Anti-counterfeiting for yoga mats

Liforme is adored by their customers for their premium yoga mats with the patented "AlignForMe" system. When they found a counterfeiting problem in their supply chain, they turned to using secure QR codes

Liforme is a London-based lifestyle company that produces premium, sustainably produced yoga mats. Their customers are fanatic about their products, and rightly so. Unfortunately, with great products come great reproductions. As soon as the team at Liforme discovered poor-quality counterfeit mats infringing on their trademark in the People’s Republic of China, they quickly prepared a mitigation plan that included applying Scantrust secure QR codes to their product packaging. This added a high-performing and easy to use anti-counterfeiting feature to every Liforme mat.

Scantrust secure QR codes gave Liforme customers a way to check product authenticity with nothing more than a mobile phone scan. This same code was also used to activate a track and trace function for anyone scanning to reveal the journey of the yoga mats once the products left Liforme warehouses.
