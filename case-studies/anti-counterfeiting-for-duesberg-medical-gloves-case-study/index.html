Anti-counterfeit solution for Duesberg medical gloves

Duesberg sought to give their customers confidence in the quality and authenticity of their medical gloves as counterfeit PPE items flooded the market during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Key features and benefits

  • Secure QR codes on tamper-evident security labels for anti-counterfeiting
  • Visual drag-and-drop landing page builder
  • Online dashboard and alerts for counterfeit product detection
  • SKU, lot, and quality assurance testing certificate data integration with mobile landing pages
  • Collection of end-user demographic data: gender, geo, product preferences, etc.

Solutions used

  • Product digital identity
  • Anti-counterfeiting

Project highlights and results

Scantrust anti-counterfeit solutions continue to mitigate the flood of counterfeit PPE products that entered the market with the COVID-19 pandemic. Duesberg uses a connected packaging anti-counterfeiting solution to ensure that anyone, anywhere in the world can be confident of the quality and authenticity of their medical gloves.
