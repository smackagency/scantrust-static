QR code track and trace with anti-counterfeit packaging for Rémy Cointreau

Rémy Cointreau uses tamper-proof, secure QR code for track and trace and anti-counterfeit packaging on bottles & cases.

Key features and benefits

  • Tamper-proof, secure QR code for anti-counterfeit packaging applied to bottles & cases
  • Enterprise data and supply chain tasks app for warehouse, wholesalers, and distributors to access and edit data fields associated with QR codes on bottles and cases
  • SAP supply chain data linked using QR codes
  • Creation and management of mobile landing pages using a drag-and-drop landing page creator
  • Mobile landing pages link with and integrate Rémy Cointreau’s social media accounts

Solutions used

  • Product digital identity
  • Anti-counterfeiting
  • Customer engagement
  • Supply chain traceability

Project highlights and results

Rémy Cointreau relies on Scantrust for supply chain traceability data and anti-counterfeit packaging with secure QR codes. Each scan of a secure QR code on Rémy Cointreau bottles is analyzed for authenticity and checked for anomalies that can indicate if the product is in the wrong geographical market. Bottle and case labels with codes were applied in-line at the warehouse during packing for distribution.
