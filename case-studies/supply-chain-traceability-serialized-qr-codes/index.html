Supply chain traceability for automotive batteries: VARTA® tracks route-to-market

Clarios sought to improve downstream traceability, and establish direct engagement with end-users of VARTA® automotive batteries.

Key features and benefits

  • Unique QR codes engraved on every battery
  • Linking of supply chain data to products before export
  • Battery-to-pallet aggregation for granular supply chain traceability

Solutions used

  • Product digital identity
  • Customer engagement
  • Supply chain traceability

Project highlights and results

Varta® automotive batteries are tracked from production to distribution and through to end-use using serial QR codes. Supply chain traceability with codes generated using the Scantrust serial QR code generator gives Varta® automotive batteries parent company Clarios the data and business intelligence they sought on battery lifecycles, supply chain structure, and product traceability.
