Scantrust Farm-to-Consumer Traceability is Available on the SAP® App Center

By integrating with SAP Logistics Business Network, material traceability option, Scantrust’s solution for Unique QR Codes for Farm-to-Consumer Traceability delivers trusted connection to end consumers

Lausanne, Switzerland — July 22, 2020 — Scantrust SA announced that its solution for Unique QR Codes for Farm-to-Consumer Traceability, part of the Scantrust Connected Goods Platform, is now available for online purchase on SAP® App Center, the digital marketplace for SAP partner offerings. The Scantrust platform integrates with the SAP Logistics Business Network, material traceability option, and delivers business productivity in the area of consumer goods with the ability to create a trusted connection to end consumers.

Scantrust Unique QR Codes for Farm-to-Consumer Traceability is available now for businesses using SAP solutions in the consumer goods industry. By implementing this solution, brands can deliver compelling stories to their consumers around the provenance of their products using unique QR codes on packaging. This is a fundamental step toward regaining consumer trust and loyalty. Every interaction with the QR codes generates valuable data and insights on consumer behavior which contribute to marketing efforts and will help brands better understand their consumers. With the unique QR code on products, a direct communication channel with consumers is made available, including the chance to obtain product feedback, offer to onboard to a loyalty program, and more. Consumers are empowered to verify the origin of the products they purchase and make more informed consumption choices.
