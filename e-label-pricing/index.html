How many e-labels do I need?

Each e-label is tied to a specific SKU (Stock Keeping Unit). Units of the same SKU can make use of the same e-label, even if sold in different countries. Therefore, the amount of e-labels that you need is equal to the amount of SKU’s, or unique products that you have.
