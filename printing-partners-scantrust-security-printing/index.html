How does Scantrust help printing and packaging partners?

Scantrust secure QR codes expand the services you already offer with the latest in cutting-edge connected packaging via QR codes.

With Scantrust, you can provide new solutions to your customers including:

Anti Counterfeiting

Apply Scantrust Secure QR codes to your customers product packaging to provide a trustworthy, quick, and user friendly way for end-customer or inspector product authentication. Also gain access to counterfeit reports and real-time scanning data in the Scantrust dashboard

Supply Chain Traceability

With our secure QR codes on product packaging, customers can tag and monitor products throughout the supply chain and access to real time product traceability data and management with Scantrust dashboard

Regulation compliance

Use QR codes to be in compliance with regional and country-level regulations like the E.U. Digital product passport or E.U. wine label regulations (link). Update your dynamic content in real time and enjoy multilingual content support

Customer engagement

Incorporate warranty registration, loyalty programs and other customer engagement metrics on your packaging. Instantly launch highly customizable and dynamic product landing pages and run global, regional, and language-based campaigns

How do I integrate with Scantrust?

Step 1: Choose a compatible printer

Compatible printing equipment:

-Digital presses
-Flexo
-Offset
-Industrial Inkjets

Incompatible printing equipment:

-Office printers
-Home printers

Step 2: Calibrate your printing equipment

After choosing the right printer, the equipment must be calibrated to be in sync with the Scantrust platform. To do this:

  1. Register on the Scantrust Printer portal. Click here to register.
  2. Order your calibration device. We’ll ship you a mobile phone and a lens which can be used to calibrate your printing equipment with.
  3. Add your equipment on the Scantrust portal and download your proof sheet. This is a simple process we will guide you through.
  4. Calibrate your equipment. Print the proof sheet on desired substrates and scan the printed codes with the calibration device. We’ll help you through this process.

Step 3: Print secure QR codes

1. Receive codes generated from BO

2. Generate and download the CSV with codes

3. Print QR codes and assure quality
