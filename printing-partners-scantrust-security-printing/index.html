Technical requirements for Scantrust printing partners

Can you print secure QR codes with your equipment?

Digital presses with a resolution of 600 DPI and above are generally suitable for printing Scantrust secure QR codes. The requirements for your exact model may vary. In some cases a higher minimum DPI threshold will apply.

 

Flexo, offset printers, and industrial inkjets are also suitable to satisfy requirements for Scantrust printing partners. To receive specific parameters related to your existing equipment, just contact our team.

 

NB: Office and home printers are not supported by Scantrust.

Steps for calibrating your equipment

  1. Register on the Scantrust Printer portal.
  2. Order a calibration device from Scantrust. To complete the required calibration you’ll need a mobile phone and lens kit. Scantrust will ship this upon confirming your order.
  3. Register your equipment in the Scantrust portal to download your proof sheet. The process is simple and we’ll help you every step of the way.
  4. Calibrate your equipment. Print the proof sheet on the desired substrates and scan the printed codes using the calibration device.

Steps for printing Scantrust secure QR codes

  1. Download the necessary files. You’ll receive access to the secure QR code files once your project is approved.
  2. Generate and download the code association CSV file. This step is necessary when the secure QR codes are associated with unique serial numbers and is used in the variable data printing process.
  3. Print the secure QR codes and validate. Once the secure QR codes are printed, they will need to be validated as “active”. Validation is typically performed by machine scanning the printed codes and can happen before or after they have been applied to items such as packaging.
